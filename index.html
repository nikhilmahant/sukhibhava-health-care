About Dr. Sudheendra Huddar

Dr. Sudheendra Huddar is a highly qualified psychiatrist based in Hubli, with a passion for helping patients overcome mental health challenges. With over 7 years of clinical experience, he specializes in psychiatry and addiction psychiatry, offering empathetic and non-judgmental care.

  • M.D. (Psychiatry) - National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), Bengaluru
  • DM (Addiction Psychiatry) - Centre for Addiction Medicine, NIMHANS

Experience

  • Over 7 years of intensive clinical work in psychiatry
  • Trained at prestigious institutes like NIMHANS, handling a wide variety of cases
  • Experience in training postgraduates, therapists, and mental health professionals

Approach to Care

Dr. Sudheendra evaluates symptoms with an open mind, striving to be a compassionate and empathetic listener. His goal is to relieve patients' symptoms using all possible approaches, ensuring personalized and effective treatment plans.
